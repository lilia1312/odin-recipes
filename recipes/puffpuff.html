Puff-Puff Recipe

Puff-Puff is a native popular nigerian snack that is mostly used as "small chops"

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Warm Water
  • Sugar
  • Nutmeg
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast: Mix together the yeast and warm water. Set it aside for a couple of minutes
  2. In a bowl, add flour, sugar, salt(optional) and nutmeg(optional only if hate flavour).
  3. Mix the flour and yeast mixture together
  4. Mix properly seriously, slap it if necessary
  5. Let the mixture rise for 30 minutes to 1 hour
  6. Pre-heat the oil, oil should be minimum 2.5 inches (just eyeball it)
  7. Make sure it is hot enough before frying
  8. Fry the puff puff, you could use your hands or a spoon
  9. Fry till golden brown on both sides except you like it burnt
